02
Styrofoam Biodegradation Study
Research Data Analysis Published
2023
↗

The Question

Which mealworm species — Tenebrio molitor or Zophobas atratus — is more effective at decomposing styrofoam waste? A small question with surprisingly real implications for waste management.

My Role

Co-author. Experimental design, daily mass measurements, statistical analysis using the Waste Reduction Index, and writeup. Published in the SMA Kolese De Britto repository.

Findings

  • Zophobas atratus: WRI 2.52, efficiency 0.193%
  • Tenebrio molitor: WRI 1.77, efficiency 0.141%
  • Statistically significant difference favouring Z. atratus
  • Top 20 out of 100 candidates recognition
